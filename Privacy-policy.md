# Privacy Policy for WorldTime

**Last updated:** April 3, 2026

## Overview

WorldTime is a timezone app that helps you track time across different cities. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

### Location Data

WorldTime requests access to your device's location to automatically detect your current city and timezone. This is optional — you can deny location access and manually add cities instead.

**Important:** Your location data is processed entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your location to any server.

### Cities You Add

The cities you add to WorldTime are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. WorldTime does not contain analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.

## Third-Party Services

WorldTime does not use any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

WorldTime does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new version here with an updated date.
